Descriptions

Zen Rabbits

Zen Rabbits live in the Japanese garden near the top of campus. They love to meditate in the Japanese garden.

Gym Rabbits

Gym Rabbits can be found bench pressing carrots at the gym. For protein, they also consume plenty of carrots.

The Wilds

The Wilds can be found roaming around without a care. They are bound only by their one supreme God.
